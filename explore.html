SHOPPING

Find everything you need during your stay at our various grocery and convenience stores.

SUPERMARKETS

Two supermarkets offer a wide selection of groceries, including local and imported products.

  • Island Market (Taniti City)
  • Bay Supermarket (Merriton Landing)

GROCERY STORES

Two smaller grocery stores provide convenient shopping with essential items.

  • Village Grocer (West Side)
  • Harbor Market (Near Port)

CONVENIENCE STORE

One 24-hour convenience store ensures you can get necessities any time of day or night.

  • Always Open (Taniti City Center)

Note: Most stores accept major credit cards and U.S. dollars. Many also accept euros and yen.

MERRITON LANDING SPOTLIGHT

The Hub of Entertainment and Activities

Merriton Landing, located on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay, is a rapidly developing area that has become the center of entertainment on Taniti. This walkable district features:

  • Microbrewery and pubs
  • New dance club
  • Water activity operators
  • Restaurants and cafes
  • Shopping opportunities
  • Upcoming golf course

The area is easily accessible by public transportation and is within walking distance of several accommodations.
